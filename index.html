Quotes and Dashes

Foot marks, inch marks, single- and double-quotes

I stand 5'7" (right)

I do not stand at 5’7” (wrong)

"Hark, who goes there," the dude said. (wrong)

“Hark, who goes there,” the dude said. (right)

"'Hark, who goes there,' the dude said." (wrong)

“‘Hark, who goes there,’ the dude said.” (right)

Dashes: em- and en-dashes

Mon – Fri (right)

Mon-Fri (wrong)

9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (right)

9:00 AM-5:00 PM (wrong)

“Computers are awesome — as are squirrels!”

“Computers — and squirrels — are awesome!”

—Roy

Figure Dashes

(212) 123-4567 (wrong)

(212) 123‒4567 (right)
